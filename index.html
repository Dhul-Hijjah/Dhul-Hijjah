Why is it Important?

The first 10 days of Dhul Hijjah are considered to be the best 10 days of the year, a time of amplified reward and mercy. During these 10 days, Muslims witness the day of Arafah, Hajj, and Eid ul-Adha. You can make the most of the blessed 10 days of Dhul Hijjah by increasing your worship and remembrance of Allah SWT.
